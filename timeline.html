Timeline — Dating of Pre‑Qur'anic Sources

A chronological timeline (approximate dates) for the extra‑Qur'anic texts and traditions that parallel specific Surah references in the Qur’an. Dates follow the attributions used in the source document; many items are approximate or debated by scholars.

c. 460 BCE – 140 CE

Book of Jubilees — Abraham’s rejection of idols

Jewish rewriting of Genesis and Exodus. Provides background for Abraham’s confrontation with idolatry.

Qur’anic parallel: Surah 6:74–83; Surah 21:51–67.

1st century CE

Life of Adam and Eve

Apocryphal expansion on Adam and Eve. Contains Satan/Iblis refusing to bow.

Qur’anic parallel: Surah 2:34; Surah 7:11–18; Surah 20:116–117.

c. 70–150 CE

Apocalypse of Abraham

Jewish apocalyptic text. Abraham’s vision and rejection of idolatry.

Qur’anic parallel: Surah 6:74–83; Surah 19:41–50.

c. 2nd century CE

Rabbinic Midrash (Pirkei DeRabbi Eliezer)

Contains story of Cain and Abel’s burial, shown by a raven.

Qur’anic parallel: Surah 5:27–31.

c. 145 CE

Protoevangelium of James

Story of Mary’s upbringing in the temple with miraculous provision.

Qur’anic parallel: Surah 3:35–37.

c. 150–200 CE

Infancy Gospel of Thomas & Apocalypse of Peter

Jesus forms birds from clay (Infancy Gospel). Substitution in crucifixion (Apocalypse of Peter).

Qur’anic parallel: Surah 3:49; Surah 4:157.

c. 3rd century CE

Alexander Romance

Legends of Alexander the Great building barriers against barbarous tribes.

Qur’anic parallel: Surah 18:83–98 (Dhul‑Qarnayn and Gog & Magog).

c. 4th century CE

Targum Sheni of Esther

Expands the Queen of Sheba story with Solomon’s control over spirits and animals.

Qur’anic parallel: Surah 27:17–44.

c. 5th – 6th century CE

Seven Sleepers of Ephesus tradition

Christian martyr tale about youths sleeping in a cave for centuries.

Qur’anic parallel: Surah 18:9–26.

c. 6th century CE

Martyrs of Najran

Accounts of Christians martyred in Arabia, remembered in later tradition.

Qur’anic parallel: Surah 85:4–8.

c. 6th – 7th century CE

Pseudo‑Gospel of Matthew

Contains the palm‑tree miracle at Mary’s labor.

Qur’anic parallel: Surah 19:22–26.

c. 7th century CE

Arda Viraf Namag & Merkabah literature

Visionary ascent texts that influenced ideas of heavenly journeys.

Qur’anic parallel: Surah 17:1 (Isra’ and Miʿrāj).

Note: Surah references are approximate and correspond to passages where parallels have been identified by scholars. Many texts have complex redaction histories; dates remain debated.
