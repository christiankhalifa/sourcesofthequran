Errors Found Within the Qur'an

1. Contradictions with the Bible

a) Names Confused:
Sura 6:84–86 lacks chronological order, confusing historical timelines of biblical figures.
b) Moses:
Sura 28:9 says Pharaoh's wife adopted Moses, contradicting Exodus 2:10 where it was Pharaoh's daughter.
c) Yahya:
Sura 19:7 states no one was named Yahya before John the Baptist, yet the name appears in 2 Kings 25:23.
d) Trinity:
Sura 5:116 misrepresents the Trinity as Father, Mary, and Son—a view never held by mainstream Christianity.
e) Man's Greatness:
Sura 4:59 bases greatness on size, which contradicts Genesis 1:27 where man's value lies in being made in God's image.

2. Internal Contradictions

a) Mary & Imran:
The Qur’an confuses Mary (mother of Jesus) with Miriam (sister of Aaron).
b) Haman:
Haman appears in Pharaoh’s court in the Qur’an, yet historically he served under the Persian king Xerxes.

3. Errors vs. Secular and Scientific Data

a) Ishmael:
Historical Arab ancestry traces to Qahtan, not Ishmael.
b) Samaritan:
Sura 20 mentions Samaritans during Moses’ time, centuries before Samaritans existed (post-722 BC).
c) Sunset:
Sura 18:86 says the sun sets in a muddy spring—mythological and scientifically inaccurate.
d) Issa:
The Qur’anic name for Jesus (Issa) is etymologically incorrect; it means Esau, not Jesus.
e) Mountains:
Suras describe mountains as stabilizers for the Earth, contrary to geological science.
f) Mathematical Inconsistencies:
Inheritance laws in Sura 4 mathematically exceed 100% of an estate.
g) Alexander the Great:
Sura 18 credits Alexander with building a giant wall—an unverified historical claim.
h) Semen and the Ribs:
Sura 86:5-7 wrongly claims semen comes from between the ribs and loins.

4. Absurdities and Legends

a) Seven Earths:
Sura 65:12 claims God created seven earths—an unverified and puzzling assertion.
b) Meteors and Jinn:
Various suras say meteors are missiles to chase jinn, contradicting astronomy.
c) Solomon and Animals:
Birds, ants, and jinn are shown talking and working for Solomon—fantastical claims with no parallel in Biblical texts.
d) Youth Sleep 309 Years:
A story in Sura 18 parallels an apocryphal Christian tale with supernatural sleep and preservation.
e) People Turned into Apes:
Suras 2 and 7 describe Jews being transformed into apes—an uncorroborated claim.
f) Jacob's Senses:
Jacob smells Joseph from miles away and regains sight from touching his coat—Sura 12:93-96.

5. Grammatical Errors

a) Tense Issues:
Sura 3:59 uses present tense "Kun feekunu" where past tense is appropriate.
b) Gender Agreement:
Sura 4:162 has feminine plurals where masculine is needed.
c) Numerical Agreement:
Sura 7:160 uses feminine numbers for masculine subjects.
d) General Grammar:
Over 100 grammatical irregularities have been cataloged by Arabic linguists.
